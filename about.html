Hey lovers!

We're Glambient - An LGBT owned business providing the most stylish photography and videography service.

If you prefer a more artistic approach, then you have come to the right people. We're creative directors, outside of the wedding industry we shoot music videos, editorials, artworks and campaigns; often on request we enjoy incorporating a magazine feeling to your party pictures and evening edit.

We are obsessed with the details… and part of that is making sure we're in the right place at the right time, so no moments are missed. We get that you will have enough to think about on the day, and sadly, there will be moments you do miss, but that's where we've got your back.

For us, those candid and fleeting moments shared between family, friends, loved ones and plus ones, is really what gives us the spark. It doesn't matter if you're camera-shy; it's our job to remain unobtrusive and preserve those natural moments that hold so much love. They catch our attention like fireflies so we're always there to capture them.

We're here to give you back those memories in full capacity. Encapsulating the energy and atmosphere of the day with a raw and cinematic feel. Though everything has a price, we build the vision together and form a genuine relationship along the way.

We love the process of building a creative journey with each couple we work with, and love to meet new characters and delve into different love stories. That's why we do what we do! Being part of your wedding isn't just another job; it's a privilege to contribute to one of the most significant chapters of your love story. And that means a lot to us.
