Meet Joey and Hunter

Hey lovers, We're The Wedding Crashers - An LGBT owned company providing the most stylish photography and videography service. If you prefer an artistic approach then you have came to the right people. We're creative directors, outside of the wedding industry we shoot music videos, fashion shoots and campaigns.

We are obsessed with the details. ensuring everything is in the right place at the right time, with the perfect lighting and moment to capture. We want you to relax and fully enjoy your wedding day.

Having a good eye for photography is one thing, but as creative directors, nothing stands in the way of capturing the dream shot. It's important to us that the art we create feels atmospheric, tender, and raw.

For us, true emotion, those candid moments shared between lovers and loved ones... is completely breathtaking to us. It doesn't matter if you're camera-shy; it's our job to remain unobtrusive and preserve those natural moments that hold so much love.

Our goal is always to give you those moments back. These are memories to keep forever, beautifully framed. A gallery of photos that tells your story. Your new favourite romance film, ready to be watched throughout your lifetime together.

What we offer isn't just a package...it's a creative journey. We build the vision together and form a genuine relationship along the way. Being part of your wedding isn't just another job; it's a privilege to contribute to one of the most significant chapters of your love story. And that means a lot to us.
